# Logout Process Guide

## Step-by-Step Process

1. **Access Profile Menu**
   - In the dashboard, at the bottom corner of the side menu, click on the tutor’s avatar or name.

2. **Logout Option**
   - A pop-up will appear with options: "My Profile" and "Logout".
   - Click on "Logout".

3. **Successful Logout**
   - After clicking "Logout", you will be successfully logged out and redirected to the landing page.
