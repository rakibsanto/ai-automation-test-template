# Tutor Availability & Session Setup Guide

## 1. Access the Website
- Go to: https://mehadedu.com/en/dashboard/availability  
- From the header section, click on the **"Become a Tutor"** button.

---

## 2. Start Tutor Application
- Scroll down slightly.
- Click on the **"Apply Now"** button.

---

## 3. Login Process
- A tutor login modal will appear.
- Click on the **country code box** and select your country code.
- Enter a valid phone number (within 12 digits).
- Click on the **"Send Code"** button.
- Enter the OTP: `123456`
- Click on the **"Continue"** button.

✅ If login is successful, the dashboard will be displayed.

---

## 4. Availability Calendar Feature
Inside the dashboard, you will find the **"Availability Calendar"** feature.

There are two options:
- **One-to-One Session**
- **Group Session**

---

## 5. Create One-to-One Session
- Click on **"Add Availability Time"**
- Select:
  - Date
  - Day
  - Time
- Click on the **"Apply"** button

✅ Your one-to-one session will be created successfully.

---

## 6. Create Group Session
- Click on the **"Group Session"** button
- A modal will appear

### Step 1: Basic Information
- Select:
  - Date
  - Day
  - Time
- Click **"Next"**

### Step 2: Course Details
- Enter **Course Name**
- Set:
  - Maximum number of students
  - Minimum number of students
  - Course price
- Click **"Next"**

### Step 3: Review Details
- Review all the entered information carefully
- Make sure all details are correct

### Final Step
- Click on **"Create Group Session"**

✅ Your group session will be created successfully.

---

## Notes
- Ensure all inputs are valid before submission.
- Double-check all session details before creating.
