```markdown id="notifications-management-md"
# Notifications Management Process

## Login Process

1. Open the browser and go to:  
   [Super Admin Dashboard](https://dev.mehadedu.com/ar/super-admin-login)

2. Login successfully using Super Admin credentials.

3. After successful login, the dashboard interface will appear.

---

## Notifications Management

1. From the sidebar menu, click on the **Notifications** button.

2. Verify that the Notifications page is displayed successfully.

3. Verify that teacher profile update/change notifications are shown correctly.

4. Verify that the system displays which tutor updated or submitted profile information.

---

## Notification Tabs Verification

Verify the following tabs:
- All
- Unread

### All Tab
1. Click on the **All** tab.
2. Verify that all stored notifications are displayed.

### Unread Tab
1. Click on the **Unread** tab.
2. Verify that only unread notifications are displayed.

---

## Tutor Details Navigation

1. Click on any unread notification.

2. Verify that the Tutor Details/Profile page opens successfully.

3. Verify that all tutor profile information is displayed correctly.

---

## Instructor Profile Approval Block

1. Verify that the right-side approval card/block is displayed.

2. Verify that pending profile change requests are shown inside the approval block.

3. Verify that a **View** button is available for each pending request.

---

## View Button Functionality

1. Click on the **View** button.

2. Verify that a modal appears with the submitted information details.

3. Verify that the modal contains:
   - Approve Button
   - Reject Button

---

## Approve Functionality

1. Click on the **Approve** button.

2. Verify that the tutor information is updated successfully.

3. Verify that the approved information becomes publicly visible.

4. Verify that students can view the updated information on the tutor profile.

---

## Reject Functionality

1. Click on the **Reject** button.

2. Verify that the system asks for a reject reason.

3. Enter a valid reject reason and submit.

4. Verify that the tutor can see the rejection reason.

5. Verify that rejected information is not publicly visible.

6. Verify that students cannot see rejected profile changes.

---

## Public Visibility Verification

1. Approve a tutor profile change request.

2. Verify that the updated information is publicly visible.

3. Reject a tutor profile change request.

4. Verify that rejected information is not publicly visible.

5. Verify that students can only see approved changes.
```
