# Message Interaction Guide

## Step-by-Step Process

1. **Tutor Login**
   - Go to `mehadedu.com`.
   - Follow the tutor login process (select country code, enter number, verify OTP, etc.).
   - Once logged in, you will reach the dashboard.

2. **Access Message Dashboard**
   - From the dashboard, click on the "Message" button.
   - A new message dashboard will open. Here, all conversations (with both teachers and students) will be displayed.

3. **Viewing Conversations**
   - Click on any conversation to open it.
   - A chat box will appear, showing the full conversation history.

4. **Check Message Delivery**
   - Verify that all messages are being sent correctly.
   - Check that plain text, special characters, and attachments are all properly transmitted.

5. **Inbox Navigation**
   - Confirm that switching between different conversations works smoothly (i.e., the view updates correctly).

6. **Unread Messages**
   - Check that when you open a conversation from the "Unread" tab, it marks that message as read.

Ensure all these processes work seamlessly before finalizing!
