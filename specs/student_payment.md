# Payment Process Automation Steps - MehaEdu
https://dev.mehadedu.com/en/dashboard/wallet

## Steps to Reproduce Payment Flow

### 1. Navigate to Website
- Open the MehaEdu website in your browser.

### 2. Student Login
- Log in as a student using valid credentials.

### 3. Find a Tutor or Course
- After login, go to the "Find Tutor" section.
- Search for a tutor or course by browsing the available options.

### 4. Select a Tutor Session
- From the search results, click on "Book Lesson" for the desired tutor.

### 5. Choose a Package
- After clicking "Book Lesson," you will see available packages.
- Select the package you wish to purchase.

### 6. Enter Payment Information
- After selecting the package, fill in the required payment details:
  - Cardholder Name
  - Card Number
  - Expiry Date
  - Security Code (CVV)

### 7. Click "Pay Now"
- After entering the payment information, click the "Pay Now" button.

### 8. Submit Confirmation
- A modal will appear for confirmation.
- Click "Submit" to finalize the payment.

### 9. Verify Payment Status
- After successful payment, go to your student profile sidebar.
- In the sidebar menu, click on the "Payments" tab.
- Verify that the payment is recorded as successful and the amount is deducted.
