# Tutor Review and Ratings Process - MehaEdu
https://dev.mehadedu.com/en/dashboard/reviews

## Steps to Reproduce Review and Ratings Flow

### 1. Navigate to Website
- Open the MehaEdu website in your browser.

### 2. Student Login
- Log in as a student using valid credentials.

### 3. Navigate to Review and Ratings
- After login, go to the site menu (sidebar) and select the "Review and Ratings" feature.

### 4. View Completed Sessions
- Under the "Review and Ratings" section, you will see a list of all completed tutor sessions.

### 5. Select a Session to Review
- Choose a completed session from the list.
- Click on the "Complete Session" button in "My Bookings."

### 6. Submit a Rating and Review
- After clicking "Complete Session," a prompt will ask for a tutor review.
- Provide the rating (number of stars) and any written feedback.

### 7. View Ratings on the Review Page
- Once submitted, return to the "Review and Ratings" page.
- Check your rating and review listed alongside the respective tutor.

---

## Notes
- Ensure each session is fully completed before submitting a review.
- Verify that the rating and review appear correctly on the summary page.
- Test that all star ratings and written feedback are saved and displayed accurately.
