# Tutor Login and Earnings/Payouts Guide

## 1. Tutor Login Process
- Go to `mehadedu.com`.
- Click on the "Become a Tutor" button in the header.
- Scroll down and select the "Apply Now" button.
- A tutor login modal will appear.
- Click on the country code box, select the appropriate code, and enter your 12-digit valid number.
- Submit and verify OTP to complete the login.

## 2. Dashboard Access
- Once logged in, you will land on the dashboard.
- Navigate to the "Earnings and Payouts" section.

## 3. Earnings Overview
- In the Earnings section, you will see your total earnings.
- Check the "Available Balance" which reflects the amount you can withdraw.
- Below that, view "Pending Earnings"—these are earnings awaiting course completion before being added to your available balance.

## 4. Complete Payouts
- In the "Complete Payouts" section, you will see all approved payments by the admin.
- Confirm that the recent payments are accurately displayed.

## 5. Validation Steps
- Ensure total earnings are correct and up-to-date.
- Verify that available balance matches the withdrawable amount.
- Check that pending earnings are correctly listed and only move to available balance once the course is completed.
- Verify that all completed payouts are shown under the "Complete Payouts" section.
- Check that admin-approved payments appear correctly in the payout section.

Ensure that all these steps are working smoothly before moving forward with any further payout requests!
