# MehadEdu Tutor Application Process

1. First, go to the [MehadEdu](https://mehadedu.com) website.  
2. From the header section, click on the "Become a Tutor" button.  
3. After clicking, scroll down, and you will see an "Apply Now" button.  
4. Click on the "Apply Now" button.  
5. Once clicked, a login dashboard will open.  
6. From there, select your country code using the dropdown menu.  
7. After selecting the country code, enter a valid number (there is a digit validation, which must be 12 digits—no more, no less).  
8. Click on the "Send Code" button.  
9. After clicking, you will receive an OTP code. The OTP is 123456.  
10. Use this OTP, and then click the "Continue" button to proceed.  
11. Once you log in successfully, you will move to the next step.

12. After logging in with the OTP, you will be presented with a signup form.  
13. In the form, enter your personal information:  
    - First Name: This field must be a maximum of 30 characters.  
    - Last Name: This field must also be a maximum of 30 characters.  
    - Email: Enter a valid email (it must be unique; you cannot use an email already used in this project).  
      The email must be a maximum of 254 characters.  
14. Next, you will fill out a bio:  
    - Bio: This is a text box with a maximum of 500 characters.  
15. Then, you will select languages:  
    - Language: Choose from the available options.  
      You can select as many as you like, but ensure the number of selections is counted.  
16. Timezone: This will be auto-selected; you don't need to modify it.  
17. Upload an introduction video:  
    - The video must be a maximum of 100 MB.  
18. Once uploaded, click the "Next" button to proceed.

19. After uploading the video, click the "Next" button.  
20. A new form will open: the Educational Certificate Form.  
21. In this form, enter your highest degree:  
    - Highest Degree: Add your highest degree (e.g., B.Sc., M.Sc.), with a maximum of 50 characters.  
22. Enter the university or institute name:  
    - University/Institute: Add the name with a maximum of 50 characters.  
23. Graduation Year:  
    - Enter the year you graduated; this must be a 4-digit number.  
24. Certificate:  
    - Upload your certificate (image or PDF) from your local computer.  
      The size must be a maximum of 10 MB.  
25. After completing these fields, click the "Next" button to proceed.

26. Next, go to the Subject and Expertise page.  
27. Here, select the subject name from a dropdown list, which contains various subjects.  
28. After selecting the subject, choose your hourly rate:  
    - The hourly rate must be between 0 and 10,000 (max 10K).  
29. About this subject:  
    - Enter a description in the textbox, with a maximum of 500 characters.  
30. Experience:  
    - Select your experience from a dropdown, between 1 and 10 years.  
31. Teaching Level:  
    - Choose the teaching level from the dropdown (Primary, Middle, High School, or University).  
32. Experience Field:  
    - Enter details in the experience field (max 1000 characters).  
33. Once completed, click the "Next" button to proceed.

34. After clicking the "Next" button, you will see a review page showing all the information you added.  
35. Check all the details carefully—personal information, education, certificates, and subject expertise.  
36. Ensure all the information is correct and displayed properly.  
37. Once everything is verified, click the "Submit Application" button.  
38. After clicking "Submit Application," your tutor application will be fully completed.
